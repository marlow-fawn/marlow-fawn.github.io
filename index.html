Hobbies

Music

I write, perform, record, and produce my own music on a variety of instruments. While a vast majority of my work is for passion's sake (see my bandcamp for reference), I have also taken commissions for projects.

Woodworking & Making

In addition to software, I deal with hardware in my spare time. My maker projects are both for creativity and utility. I do a lot of woodworking in my projects, along with laser-cut pieces and integrated mechanical components.

Most of my work is pro bono, as I believe everyone deserves to have functioning, safe furniture and other home goods. In addition, almost all the materials used in my projects are recycled to reduce impact and cost.
